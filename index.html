What Batch Sells

  • Marketing tools and analytics

Critical Misconception to Address

Partners commonly believe they're paying for guaranteed bookings. They're actually paying for visibility, SEO, and traffic. This drives most cancellation requests. The team must confidently explain what partners receive.

Partner Support Team's Role (Rick's Job)

  • Act as account managers for Core/Basic tier partners
  • Educate partners on the value they receive
  • Proactively send performance emails with metrics
  • Help optimize listings (keywords, tags, descriptions)
  • Manage cancellation/churn requests
  • Handle disputes and subscription updates
  • Offer retention packages to save at-risk accounts

What Counts as "Value" for Partners

  • Page views on their listing
  • Favorites (hearts)
  • Itinerary additions
  • Referral link clicks to their website

All trackable in the analytics dashboard — even without direct bookings

SEO: The 3-Month Rule

  • SEO is a compounding investment
  • Requires 3 months to show results
  • Lock-in period exists for this reason
  • First 3 months: listing optimization phase
  • After 3 months: itinerary adds & referrals pick up

⚠️ Optimization is NOT included in Basic/Core

Optimization, blog posts, and keyword additions are not part of the base subscription. This is key — these excluded features become retention leverage when trying to save at-risk partners. Offer them as add-on value to prevent churn.

Retention Strategy

Golden Rule: Show Metrics Before Discounts

When a partner requests cancellation, first show performance data. Only offer retention packages after demonstrating value. Don't lead with discounts.

Frame positively: "You're in the top performers in your area" for strong signals.

Retention Packages (offer after showing metrics)

  • 25% discount for 3 months
  • Free month (pause subscription)
  • Monthly billing instead of quarterly
  • Blog post creation (normally excluded from Basic)
  • Listing optimization (normally excluded from Basic)
  • Keyword additions (normally excluded from Basic)

The "Delay" Strategy (Churn Prevention)

1
Pause subscription (1 free month)Buys time to demonstrate value
2
Create blog post, optimize listing, add reviewsTangible improvements during the free period
3
Schedule follow-up meetingOne week before free month ends
4
Review improved metrics togetherLet the data make the case for staying
Case Study: Las Vegas ATV Tours
  • 1.5+ year partner, requested cancellation
  • Issues: couldn't reach phone support + wrong name in blog post
  • Cliff's solution: 1 free month pause + follow-up meeting May 29 + new phone support commitment
  • Lesson: Internal improvements can be leveraged to save accounts

Refund Policy

Standard position: No refunds. Two exceptions only:

  • Exception 1: No listing was ever published
  • Exception 2: Failure to act on a documented cancellation request (charged again)

After 3-month commitment: can refund last 2 months but NOT the first (resources invested in optimization)

⚠️ Dispute Win Rate Issue

Currently winning only 6% of disputes (should be higher). Reason: partners only have a timestamp for policy acceptance, not a signed document. Stefan is implementing DocuSign/PandaDoc to fix this. Current workaround: build strong timestamp + product evidence files per Stefan's documentation.
