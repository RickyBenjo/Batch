Critical Misconception to Address in Every Churn Call

Partners believe they're paying for guaranteed bookings. They're actually paying for visibility, SEO, and traffic. This single misunderstanding drives the majority of cancellation requests. Always clarify this confidently.

What Counts as "Value" for Partners

  • Page views on their listing
  • Favorites (hearts)
  • Itinerary additions
  • Referral link clicks to their website

All visible in analytics dashboard — even without direct bookings on Batch

SEO: The 3-Month Rule

  • SEO is a compounding investment
  • Requires 3 months to show meaningful results
  • Lock-in period exists specifically for this reason
  • First 3 months: optimization phase
  • After 3 months: itinerary adds and referrals increase

Six Core Value Propositions

1. Direct Traffic

Access to Batch's 3+ million customers actively searching for experiences. Partners tap into an existing, large audience immediately.

2. Zero Commission (Batch Direct)

Referral-based subscription model means no commission on bookings for basic partners — unlike typical marketplaces at 15–30%.

3. SEO Optimization

Batch optimizes titles, descriptions, and keywords. Direct backlinks to partner websites. SEO compounds over time — long-term traffic value.

4. Targeted Exposure via Tags

Category matching drives relevant audience targeting. The right customers find the right experiences based on tags applied to listings.

5. Analytics Dashboard

Performance insights: page views, favorites, itinerary additions, referral clicks — all trackable and reportable. Shows ROI even without direct bookings.

6. Marketing Tools

UGC collection (free customer photos/videos via QR code), blog posts, content creator connections — all included in Basic/Core.

Retention Packages

  • 25% discount for 3 months
  • Free month (pause subscription)
  • Monthly billing instead of quarterly
  • Blog post creation (normally excluded)
  • Listing optimization (normally excluded)
  • Keyword additions (normally excluded)

Refund Policy

Standard: No refunds.

Two exceptions only:

  • Exception 1: No listing was ever published
  • Exception 2: Charged again after a documented cancellation request

After 3-month lock-in: refund last 2 months but NOT the first (optimization resources were invested)

The "Delay" Strategy — Most Effective Retention Approach

1
Pause subscription (1 free month)Buys time without immediate pressure to decide
2
Create blog post, optimize listing, add reviewsTangible improvements during the free period — show you're working for them
3
Schedule follow-up meetingOne week before the free month ends
4
Review improved metrics togetherLet the data make the case — partner often chooses to stay
Case Study: Las Vegas ATV Tours
  • 1.5+ year partner requested cancellation
  • Two issues: couldn't reach phone support + wrong name in blog post
  • Solution: 1 free month pause + follow-up meeting May 29 + new phone support commitment
  • Lesson: Internal improvements (like Rick joining) can be leveraged to save accounts
⚠️ Dispute Win Rate Problem

Currently winning only 6% of disputes despite being legally in the right. Reason: partners only have a policy acceptance timestamp, not a signed document — making disputes hard to defend. Stefan is implementing DocuSign/PandaDoc to fix this. Until then, build strong evidence files with timestamps for each dispute.

Listing Optimization Standards

  • Minimum 10 reviews to start (more is better)

  • FAQs sourced from partner's own website

  • Always communicate improvements back to the partner to demonstrate value
